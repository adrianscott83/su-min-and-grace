Getting There

Arriving by Tube

Willesden Junction (Bakerloo Line & Silverlink Overground) is a 5-10 minute walk away. Do not take Old Oak lane Exit, this takes you in the wrong direction. Take the Harrow Road exit and turn right then right again onto Scrubs Lane.

Kensal Green (Bakerloo Line & Silverlink Overground) is a 10-15 minute walk away. Turn right out of the station and left onto Scrubs Lane.

Arriving by Bus

Loft Studios lies on both the No. 220 and No. 18 bus routes. The No. 220 bus follows a route from Wandsworth via Hammersmith and Shepherdâ€™s Bush and drops you outside the studio. Use the Car Giant stop. The No. 18 bus from Euston and Baker Street takes you to the top of Scrubs Lane. The Harrow Road/Scrubs Lane stop is a 3 minute walk from the studio.

Arriving by Car

Loft Studios can be located by SatNav using 77-81 Scrubs Lane or NW10 6QU. Free street parking is located on adjoining residential streets.
